How to Make a Grill Cheese

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
    • Place bread in frying pan and fry
      • Add cheese on top of bread
        • Cover with second slice of bread
          • Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
